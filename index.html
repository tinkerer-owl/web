about me;

Hey, I'm Rahul, and I'm all about that Maker life. You'll usually find me knee-deep in the alternative learning scene, getting folks hands-on with some good ol' tinkering.

Now, let's talk about sustainability. Crafting anything tangible is like a big ol' investment of material and energy, right? That's why I'm all about pushing for sustainable practices. We've gotta keep our eyes on the planet, 'cause if we distance ourselves too much, we might end up in a cold, mechanized world instead of one filled with compassion.

choices we're making now in our creative ventures? They're like seeds we're planting for the future. That's why I'm all in on studying new materials and tech, but not without keeping an eye on the challenges they bring. It's a balancing act, but it's one I'm passionate about. And hey, in the midst of all that serious stuff, I still find joy in tinkering and hacking away. It's what keeps me going!
